Thank you for your interest in competing in the equestrian portion of "Lysts on the Lake 2012". Please complete the application below by filling in all the required fields. If you participated in "Lysts on the Lake 2011", we still have the information that you submitted for 2011. You may choose to use the same information submitted last year by checking: "Please use last year's information where available." However, if you would like to update the information, please feel free to do so. You will not be able to submit your application until all of the required fields have been completed.

This page will continue to be active until Jan. 6, 2012. After that, the application process will be closed. On Jan. 9, 2012 you will receive a waiver and information packet which you will be required to sign and return. Scanned signatures will be accepted and are the quickest method of returning the completed waiver packet. There are a limited number of slots available for the joust so the roster will be determined by the order in which we receive your signed waiver. The 2011 roster filled up in just a few days and there is much greater interest for 2012.

A new addition for 2012 is the "theme song". Please provide a maximum of a 30 second .mp3 of a song that you feel best represents you. We will use these sound bites to introduce you as you ride onto the field. You may use any song you like but please be respectful of the family oriented audience.
